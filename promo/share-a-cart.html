Share-A-Cart for Amazon.com

Amazon Share-A-Cart allows you to share the contents of your cart with anyone else.

Chrome Firefox

Share-A-Cart is the easiest way to share the contents of your Amazon.com shopping cart with anyone else! No more moving items from cart to wishlist and backwards, simply "Send Cart" directly to your friend via a short id code. The id code is then used to load your cart from anywhere and anyone you shared this code with. Enjoy!
