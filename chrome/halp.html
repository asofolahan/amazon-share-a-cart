Share-A-Cart for Amazon.com

Thanks for installing!

Below are the simple instructions that explain how to operate Share-A-Cart. Enjoy!

Sending Cart

Sending cart is really simple! First, shop on Amazon.com and place the items you would like to share into your cart. Don't forget to set the correct quantity as well. Once, ready, click on the extension icon and press Send Cart. The contents of your cart will be packaged for delivery to whoever you want to send it to. Once packaged, you will see your cart's id. This is the ID that you need to pass to your friend, simply IM, SMS, or email it to them!

Receiving Cart

Receiving cart is equally simple! First, make sure to get the correct cart id from your friend. Once you have it, click on the extension icon and press Receive Cart. In the "Cart ID" field, type or paste the cart id that you friend passed on to you, and when ready, click "Get Cart". Your friends cart will be retrieved, and Amazon.com shopping cart page will open up. Presto!

Notes

Share-A-Cart does not clear your cart, so if you already have some items in the shopping cart, new cart will simply be added on top of whatever you already had in it.
